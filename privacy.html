KanaConquest Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how KanaConquest LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our iOS application, KanaConquest (the "App").

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our App. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we, through our authentication provider Firebase (a Google service), may collect your email address and a user identifier. This information is used solely for account creation, authentication, and management within the App.
  • Usage Information (Non-Personal): We may collect non-personal information about your interaction with the App, such as features used and crash reports, to help us improve functionality and stability. This data is typically aggregated and anonymized.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Providing and maintaining the App's functionality.
  • Authenticating users and managing user accounts.
  • Responding to your support requests and communications.
  • Improving the App's performance, features, and user experience.
  • Complying with legal obligations.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information under the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We use Firebase for authentication. Firebase's use of your information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can learn more about Firebase's privacy practices here: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no internet or email transmission is ever fully secure or error-free.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13 (or the relevant age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
